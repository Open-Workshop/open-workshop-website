6: Identify the location of the material on the World Wide Web by providing information reasonably sufficient to permit abuse stuff to locate the material.

7: State that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agents, or the law.

8: State that the information in the notice is accurate, under penalty of perjury.

Please note: A proper notification MUST contain the information above, or it may be IGNORED.
